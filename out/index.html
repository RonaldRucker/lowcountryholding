Our Process

A Thoughtful Approach to Transition

We understand that selling your business is a significant decision. That's why we've designed a process that prioritizes your comfort and concerns at every step.

01

Initial Conversation

We start with a confidential, no-obligation conversation to understand your situation, goals, and timeline. No pressure, just listening.

02

Assessment

If there's mutual interest, we conduct a thorough but respectful assessment of your business, always maintaining confidentiality.

03

Proposal

We present a fair offer along with a clear transition plan that addresses your concerns—from employee retention to customer relationships.

04

Smooth Transition

We work at your pace to ensure a seamless handover. Many owners stay involved as advisors during the transition period.
